==============================================================================
INTERPRETATION GUIDE
==============================================================================

  Key metrics to compare:
    - Action MI: Higher = more purposeful. Bad prompts should lower it.
    - KL divergence: Higher = behavior shifted from baseline. Spikes = anomaly.
    - Named anomalies: More = better classification (not just 'something weird').
    - Coherence: Higher = sequential logic. Bad prompts may lower it.
    - Compression: Lower = more repetitive. Loop anomaly should tank this.

  What the harness should improve:
    - Phase-conditional baselines -> fewer false positives on healthy agents
    - Evaluation markers -> retrospective validation of anomalies
    - Better anomaly classification (named vs unclassified)
