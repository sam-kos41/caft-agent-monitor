CAFT → Parsimony

A construct-validation arc on AI coding-agent behavior. Empirical phase complete 2026-05-16. Every number below is reproducible from a frozen, seed-locked pipeline; the negatives are the contribution.

The project set out to build a multi-construct, human-factors-grounded monitor for AI coding agents. Disciplined validation killed most of what it set out to build and, in doing so, found something sharper and more defensible. This is the honest arc.

1 · The autopsy negative result

The original detector (CAFT) claimed per-session "health" / anomaly detection. Tracing the real pipeline and validating it against a domain expert: agreement κ = −0.04 (worse than chance). A steelman — three independent baseline designs (self-calibrating, corpus, change-point) — all failed the same way: they fire on a calibration/window-fill transient, not on session content. A lead/lag test confirmed the "early warning" was an artifact, not prescience. Per-step single-session pathology detection does not work. This was documented, not buried, and the construct was retired.

2 · The reframe

What survived: the information-theoretic metrics themselves (mutual information, compression, KL, entropy of the action sequence) are real, deterministic measurements of behavioral structure. The error was the construct on top, not the measurement. The project pivoted from per-instance detection to population-level construct validation on an external, outcome-labelled corpus (SWE-agent trajectories; test-based pass/fail — independent of any behavioral metric, so non-circular).

3 · IT validated positive result

Pre-registered pilot, frozen sample N=2000 (1000 resolved / 1000 not), symbolization gate passed (IT not a tool-API artifact).

Information-theoretic behavioral structure

H1: AUC 0.75 > permutation-null p95 0.53  ·  H2: ΔAUC +0.044 over a trivial baseline, 95% CI [+0.026,+0.062]. Modest, robust, non-artifactual, outcome-relevant.

4 · Everything else collapses into it the finding

Four further constructs were each pre-registered, gated, and tested with a locked decision rule. Each predicts the outcome on its own — and not one adds predictive value beyond IT structure (the leg-defining H3: does it beat baseline + IT?).

The agent-native construct is the sharpest case: it is the least correlated with IT (0.43, vs 0.64–0.75 for the ports) — genuinely a distinct measurement — yet still adds essentially zero predictive value beyond it. The claim is therefore not "everything correlates with IT" but the stronger: IT already captures whatever predicts this outcome; additional, even genuinely distinct, measurements do not improve prediction.

5 · It is not a binarization artifact generalization

Rival explanation: maybe the collapse was just because the outcome was blunt pass/fail. Generalization G re-ran the leg-defining tests against a graded outcome (fraction of tests passing), which adds resolving power exactly where a hidden dimension would most likely appear.

G-H1: IT predicts the graded outcome (Spearman 0.277 vs null 0.046) · G-H2: ΔSpearman +0.101, CI [+0.065,+0.166] (relatively larger than the binary effect) · G-H3: no construct re-separates (all ΔSpearman ≈ 0, CIs span 0, none survive Bonferroni). The binarization-artifact explanation is rejected on this axis.
Parsimony thesis. For predicting coding-task outcomes in agent trajectories, the information-theoretic structure of the action sequence is doing the work. Human-factors constructs and an agent-native one each carry outcome signal but reduce to it — and this holds under a graded outcome.
Scope of the claim (the boundary, stated plainly). One corpus (nebius SWE-agent trajectories), one agent family (Llama, reactive ReAct), one outcome family (SWE-bench resolution). Generalization G is conditional on a selection bias — its parseable subset over-represents successes and is valid for runs that reached test execution. One LLM-graded robustness check was inconclusive (the only available grader was too weak). "Agents are universally flat" is not claimed. Cross-architecture generalization (a planning agent vs the reactive one) is the single open axis and is deliberately deferred — it is the expensive one and not pursued.

6 · Why this is credible — the discipline

Every result above is load-bearing only because of process, not results: pre-registration committed before any data-touching code; a symbolization-audit gate + mandatory human checkpoint at every leg; label-shuffle null models for every comparison; convergent and discriminant validity; locked decision rules that executed themselves from the data with no threshold re-weighed post-hoc; honest scoping (constructs the corpus could not support — e.g. SA comprehension, wall-clock timing — were excluded with documented reasons, never faked); dated amendments for every mid-stream under-specification; test-first throughout (~1,012 tests; tests caught several real bugs before they could corrupt a result). Negatives were reported with no spin. The methodology is the reusable asset.
